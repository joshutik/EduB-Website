Course Information

Our coding club is structured to accommodate your preferences: a weekend schedule, a fast-tracked course, a self-paced learning schedule, or a standard learning schedule. The duration for either plan you choose is dependent on the course chosen. See here...(link).

We also have our coding club established in several schools across Nigeria. At the schools, our coding instructors are committed to teaching students as part of the school's curriculum to succeed at coding.

Registration: To enroll in our coding club, all you have to do is register here on our website or visit any of our offices onsite.

Frequency: Training is done on all five working days of the week for adults and teenagers, but on Wednesdays and Saturdays for kids, considering their regular school schedule. However, we have a weekend schedule as well as variety-paced learning for students. Variety learning includes fast-paced classes, self-paced classes, standard classes, and weekend classes as you would see here (link to table).

Pace/Schedule: All sessions of our courses except the fast-tracked have a duration of two hours.
